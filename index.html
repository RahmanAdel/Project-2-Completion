Features you will love & enjoy

Clients of G3 architecture firm will appreciate clear communication and regular updates on the project progress, attention to detail and creative problem-solving, flexibility and willingness to incorporate client input and preferences.

Desktop & Mobile Version

G3 architecture can provide our clients with a desktop app that includes 3D modeling, project management and CAD software. The mobile app can include features such as access to project information, messaging platform, calendar of upcoming meetings and virtual/augmented reality technology.

G3 architecture's drag and drop builder feature allows clients to easily design and make changes to their building plans. It is user-friendly, intuitive and saves time in the design process. This feature can be found in the desktop app, providing a seamless experience for clients.
